About Our Bookstore

Dear readers,

We offer a platform where users can lend books to the needful and even donate some of them. This can include a huge collection of books in diverse categories of school reference books proposed by different institutes as the syllabus across the country. Besides this, we also have the option to donate the books, so that they can be reused by someone. It will not only help people but also will help us take a step towards sustainable earth by printing less.

We attempt to extend customer satisfaction by catering to easy user-friendly search engines, quick and user-friendly payment options, and quicker delivery systems.

We humbly invite all the lenders and buyers around the country to partner with us and help us achieve a global mission. We would like to thank you for using our platform. You can write us for any new thoughts at “bookyourbook@gmail.com” helping us to improvise the reader satisfaction and lending experience.
